We're looking at some trends in global warming over time, as well as having detailed information about different areas and their yearly and total snowfall. It's interesting to see how snow falls and levels change over time, and the trends that have changed overall. Feel free to hover over different spots on the map to get more details about a region and that's snowfall.

Note: The interactions involve: a slider/button combo, to select viewing averages/trends or to select the different year the user wants to see. The maps will populate with data on tempeerature and precipitation, as well as an aggregate map. The user can mouse over different areas to see more details on the areas, and they can select areas to populate the detailed fact section about that region.
